Deliberate Perception is the practice of attending to experience sense by sense. Attention can reveal distinctions that habit misses. The practice uses the Four Windows and returns to each sense across the proposed curriculum.

To listen to a taste, or attend to the temperature and pressure of touch, is to discover fineness inside what habit had called familiar. The practice seeks a more available sensorium rather than permanent intensity.
