*“I am that, I am.”*

Coherent Resonance names a steadier relation between inner purpose and outward conduct. Inner practice can influence an encounter, but no person controls the room or remains independent of circumstance. The term describes a quality of being in this volume. It does not name a wallet or technical record.

The image is musical. A steady note can invite response without requiring every instrument to match it. Resonance is relation among differences, not the conversion of a room into one voice.
