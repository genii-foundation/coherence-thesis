Presencing is one proposed way coherence may grow between people. The currency metaphor appears elsewhere in the series, but the lived relation and its technical record are not the same thing. Here *current* names the trust and attunement that make exchange meaningful. Whether that current can be measured, recorded, or circulated without distortion remains an open design question.
