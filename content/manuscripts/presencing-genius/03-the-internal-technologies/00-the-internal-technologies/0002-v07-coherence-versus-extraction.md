Extraction isolates a living thing from its relations and treats it as a resource. The same habit can enter human encounters when we take attention, labor, or agreement without meeting the person who gives it. Coherence begins with relation. It asks what an encounter can generate without reducing either participant to a means.

Presence is the practice by which an encounter may remain available to that possibility. In this volume, *genius* names what can arise between people when neither is being used by the other. This is a wager about relation, not proof that every attentive encounter will be generative.
