Purposeful Mentorship is the proposed gathering of practitioners whose work already develops attention, relationship, or embodied awareness. They are not asked to surrender their traditions to this architecture. Any synthesis must name its sources, limits, and differences rather than absorb them under a new vocabulary.

Each Initiate is a broad container rather than a single lesson. A practitioner might locate breath work within Embodiment, deep listening within Listening, or council practice within Providence. The map could help participants find teachers while preserving the origin and integrity of each practice.
