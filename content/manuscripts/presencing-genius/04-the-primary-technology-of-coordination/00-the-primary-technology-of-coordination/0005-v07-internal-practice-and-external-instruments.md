Internal practice does not require hostility to external tools. Instruments may support reflection, accessibility, research, or communication. They may also distort a practice by turning it into a score.

The project proposes studying some physiological correlates of regulation and encounter. Those signals cannot establish wisdom, honesty, moral worth, or readiness to lead. Any research must remain voluntary, participant-controlled, culturally and clinically reviewed, and open to the possibility that the proposed measures fail. The strongest defense against charisma and group comfort is not a biometric oracle. It is a combination of evidence, plural interpretation, independent oversight, and the right to refuse.

⁂

The practices described here are unfinished. They are offered for testing, correction, and refusal. The aim is a humane technology that develops capacity without consuming people or treating the living world as an unlimited reserve. Whether this proposal can meet that aim will be decided by practice and evidence, not by the confidence of its language.
