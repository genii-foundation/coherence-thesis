Retreats would emphasize practice over information. They may let participants encounter Wholesome Eroticism, Loving Presence, or Coherent Resonance in community. Facilitators cannot promise a state or arrange conditions so that a preferred interpretation becomes compulsory.

Retreats may deepen bonds within a cohort. They can also create pressure to conform or disclose. The design must protect difference, privacy, and the freedom to leave while making room for genuine kinship to grow.
