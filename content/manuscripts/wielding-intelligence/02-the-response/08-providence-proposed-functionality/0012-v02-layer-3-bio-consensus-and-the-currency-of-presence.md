*Unit: COHERENCE. Rooted in presence and built carefully over time.*

The trust layer’s deepest extension is a self-owned record of demonstrated coherence. The later volumes call the biometric path that would inform this record **Bio-Consensus**. That path is intended to mint the **Currency of Presence**, whose unit is **COHERENCE**. The layer begins not in technology but in the oldest human reality: the real presence two people bring to each other. Measurement is only what eventually lets that presence travel. The record is opt-in and encrypted, held by the participant and never by Providence. It is never centrally aggregated or imposed as an external score. It may open doors to greater trust, but it may never close the door to survival. Participants choose whether, when, and with whom to reveal any part of it. It behaves like currency in the oldest sense: not a tradable token, but a record of demonstrated coordination capacity that circulates only when its holder chooses.
