Once aligned people can find one another, this layer addresses whether sustained cooperation can develop. It requires mechanisms for demonstrating trustworthiness over time, repairing ruptures, and building reputational signal that reflects real relational integrity rather than performance, virality, or gaming. This is the layer where the project will succeed or fail.
