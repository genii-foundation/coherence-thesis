Providence is not a builder collective, and it does not speak for anyone named in this chapter. OpenCivics, the Capital Institute, ReFi DAO, the Inner Development Goals, the Mind & Life Institute, the contemplative-sustainability researchers, the AI alignment community, and the Indigenous-led conservation initiatives are doing their own work on their own terms; the book’s mention of them is description, not affiliation. Providence is one attempt to build coordination infrastructure adequate to a field that exists with or without it, and that will continue to develop whether or not this particular project succeeds.
