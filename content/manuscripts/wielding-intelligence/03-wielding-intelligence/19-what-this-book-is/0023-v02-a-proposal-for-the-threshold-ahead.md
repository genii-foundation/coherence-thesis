The book is finished. This last note makes the invitation as direct as the conditions allow.

If you have read this far, you have already done something rare. You have held an argument across hundreds of pages that asks you to integrate physiological reality, relational dynamics, institutional analysis, economic theory, governance design, AI alignment, and civilizational transition into a single coherent picture. Most readers do not finish books like this. The fact that you have means something the book is willing to take seriously: you can see what the book has been describing, and some part of you recognizes the work being asked of you in what you can see.

This note is the specific request.
