*The Final Integration*

The book has described what humane technology would require. Not as a specification. As a substrate. Coordination infrastructure adequate to civilizational coherence. AI in service of sensemaking. Currencies that perceive what extraction cannot. Economies that circulate value back into the conditions that sustain them. Governance emerging from the quality of the relational field. Builders capable of integrating worlds modernity kept separate. People doing the inner work that complex coordination depends on.

In this book’s framing, humane technology is not a product category. It is a relationship between technology and the life that produces it. Technologies built within coherent civilizations strengthen coherence. Technologies built within extractive civilizations accelerate extraction. Technology itself is downstream of conditions it cannot generate.

*This is why the book has dwelt on conditions rather than specifications. What humane technology would be is finally a question of what humanity will become. The Epilogue is not a separate argument. It integrates what the book has been pointing toward in service of the wielding its title has named from the start.*
