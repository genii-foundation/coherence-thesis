*Presence* is an individual's availability in a given moment. It includes regulation, attention, receptivity, and the ability to remain in contact with reality rather than retreating into performance or defense. It can be cultivated. Some of its correlates may be observed in language, voice, cardiac rhythm, and pulse. None of those signals is a complete account of the person.

*Presencing* is the relational event that may occur when people meet with enough presence to create something neither brought alone. It appears in mutual discovery, co-regulation, the transmission of judgment, and the emergence of shared action. It belongs to the encounter rather than to either individual in isolation.

This distinction matters because a currency based only on private self-regulation would reward withdrawal and invite gaming. The deeper value lies in what a person helps make possible with others.

The individual's longitudinal history is called a coherence record. It is meant to remain a portrait of participation and growth, not a single score. The unit the system would eventually remember is **COHERENCE**. The Currency of Presence is the name of the larger economic idea; COHERENCE is its proposed unit.
