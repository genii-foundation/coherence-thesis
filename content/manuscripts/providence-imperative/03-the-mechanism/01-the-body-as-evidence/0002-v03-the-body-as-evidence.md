*The body is not an instrument carried by the mind. It is part of the mind's first context and continuing record.*

*After Bessel van der Kolk, The Body Keeps the Score*

The Currency of Presence depends on a difficult capability: detecting useful correlates of presence during an encounter without claiming to read a person's essence. If no such correlates can be measured reliably, Providence remains a network of human facilitation and careful matching. That work may still matter, but the proposed currency loses its technical basis.

The mechanism therefore bears a stricter burden than the surrounding vision. It must distinguish observation from inference, correlation from cause, and a physiological state from a moral judgment. The body contributes evidence. It does not deliver an oracle.
