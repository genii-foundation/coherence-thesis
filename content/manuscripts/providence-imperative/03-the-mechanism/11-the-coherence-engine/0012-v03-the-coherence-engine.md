*To be seen accurately, without flattery or judgment, is rare. A reflective instrument should deepen that human practice, not impersonate it.*

*Adapted from The Coherence Thesis, Volume II*

The Providence Coherence Engine is the proposed system for integrating the four streams. Its first task is not to mint a permanent currency. It is to produce a provisional, moment-by-moment account of an encounter that participants can inspect and correct.

The four streams, or Bio-Consensus, are the primary proposed source of **COHERENCE**. An ancillary path, called **Proof of Council** in later volumes, would recognize a gathering only after the practice of council met an agreed validation standard. The physiological path remains primary in the canon. Neither mechanism should be treated as empirically validated until its methods and consequences are tested.

A possible implementation would process each stream separately, record uncertainty, then combine the outputs. Agreement could increase confidence in a narrow observation. Disagreement should lower confidence and generate a question. If words suggest ease while cardiac data suggest activation, the system does not announce that the body has exposed a lie. It asks whether the signals have been misread, whether the person was activated for another reason, or whether something important remained unspoken.

The engine's output should preserve this humility. It maps observations and confidence, not essence.
