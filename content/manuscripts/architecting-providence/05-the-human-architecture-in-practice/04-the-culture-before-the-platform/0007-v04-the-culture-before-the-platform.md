*A platform amplifies the habits already present in the people who govern it.*

Culture lives in repeated behavior: who is heard, what earns status, how conflict is handled, and what a community celebrates or hides. The founding group cannot choose every future custom. It can establish practices that expose the difference between stated values and ordinary conduct.
