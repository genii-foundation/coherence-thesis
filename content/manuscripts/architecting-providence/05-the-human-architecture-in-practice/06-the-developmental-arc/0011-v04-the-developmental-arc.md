*No currency makes its holders better. Presence cannot be minted into character.*

*Adapted from The Coherence Thesis, Volume III*

*Participation is not a state. It is a trajectory. The institution is responsible for the trajectory, not only for the initial conditions.*

The developmental arc is a heuristic, not a career ladder, eligibility test, or universal theory of adult growth. People may enter, pause, return, contribute in one domain, or decline greater responsibility. No stage may determine subsistence, equal voice, or human worth.
