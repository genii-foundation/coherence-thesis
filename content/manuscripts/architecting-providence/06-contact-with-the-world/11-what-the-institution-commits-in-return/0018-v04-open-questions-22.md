The open questions are concrete: who receives inquiries, which opportunities exist, what participation costs, who owns the work, what safeguards apply, and what response a person can expect. The published Volume V does not answer them through an implementation report. A public participation route must.

Until those answers exist, reading this book does not enroll anyone in a network. The invitation remains an offer that a reader may test, decline, or carry elsewhere.
