*The nest was left unfinished on purpose; what remains is the part that was always meant for other hands.*

*Adapted from The Coherence Thesis, Volume III*

*An invitation is not a promise of success. It is an offer of shared commitment to the attempt.*


The invitation is not a test of character. Different parts of the work require different skills, availability, and appetite for risk. Honest participation begins with a bounded role, clear terms, and the freedom to decline.

This manuscript does not yet provide a durable public route for builders, researchers, funders, or communities to act on the invitation. Until that route exists, the invitation remains conceptual.
