Toby Ord's *The Precipice* (2020) considers decisions under uncertainty when consequences may be very large. Charles Perrow and Diane Vaughan examine how ordinary system features can produce predictable paths to disaster. Their work supports staged, reversible experiments and careful attention to near misses. It does not establish that Providence's expected benefit exceeds its risk.
