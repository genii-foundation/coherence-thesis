W3C specifications for decentralized identifiers and verifiable credentials provide standards, not a complete identity system. The Decentralized Identity Foundation and Hyperledger projects document implementation choices. Ann Cavoukian's Privacy by Design and work by the W3C Privacy Interest Group frame privacy as an architectural property rather than a policy notice.

danah boyd and Shoshana Zuboff examine how digital systems shape identity and surveillance. Their work is a reminder that technical control is incomplete if participants cannot understand the system or resist its social demands.
