*The will can compose a story. The body still deserves attention.*

*Adapted from The Coherence Thesis, Volume III*

*A piece of technology is not what it does. It is what it refuses to do. The refusals are the constitution.*


A product specification should describe limits as clearly as capabilities. Otherwise the priorities of a team, funder, or vendor become the application's unwritten constitution.
