Legibility follows power. People with prestigious affiliations and well-documented histories can produce recognized signals more easily than those whose work has gone unrecorded. A trust layer may therefore reproduce hierarchy while calling the result merit. Providence needs bias audits, alternative evidence, appeal, and equal standing that does not depend on any attestation.
