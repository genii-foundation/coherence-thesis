Five hundred is not a target to reach quickly. It is a governance load to test. Growth from a founding cohort toward a nested bioregional community would expose whether succession, conflict repair, provisioning, ecological limits, and anti-capture rules still hold.

One working Scale would not prove that the model can travel everywhere. It would provide bounded evidence from one place. Later communities would need to test what transfers, what changes, and what should be rejected.

*The first Scale, if it forms, should be a place people can visit, question, leave, and evaluate against the claims made here. Until then, it remains an architecture awaiting ground.*
