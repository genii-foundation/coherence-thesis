Success changes the risk profile. A useful institution holds assets, reputation, data, relationships, and public legitimacy. Those resources can attract acquisition, imitation, political pressure, opportunistic partnership, or internal competition for authority. The claim does not require a hidden enemy. Ordinary incentives are enough.
