The architecture must learn. Regular audits, public incident reports, independent review, sunset clauses, threat modeling, and repair procedures can reveal new failure modes. Participants need enough knowledge to challenge the system, but they should not be asked to live in permanent vigilance.

The immune system is a useful metaphor only up to a point. Critics are not pathogens. Dissent is not infection. A community that mistakes discomfort for attack will destroy the trust it means to protect.
