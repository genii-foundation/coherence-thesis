*A safeguard changes the behavior of anyone who wants to evade it.*

Capture is not static. Funders, operators, political actors, vendors, and participants can learn how safeguards work and search for a path around them. A serious design therefore needs a threat model that can change without treating every critic or newcomer as an enemy.
