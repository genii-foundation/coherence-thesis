*The offer is five million dollars from a wealthy reader who wants the project to succeed. The donor is not a villain. The scenario matters because the gift is offered in good faith.*

*There are three conditions. The community must grow from eighty-seven people toward several hundred within three years. The donor receives a governance seat. Public accounts associate the project closely with the donor's support.*

*Each condition has a plausible rationale: greater reach, useful judgment, and ordinary recognition. Together they would let money set the pace, purchase authority, and shape public identity.*
