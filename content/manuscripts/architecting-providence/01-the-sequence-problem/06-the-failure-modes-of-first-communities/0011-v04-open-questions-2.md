The first community will adapt the architecture to a particular place, law, culture, and group. It still needs a review process that can distinguish local adaptation from the erosion of constitutional commitments.

Scale also remains open. This volume distinguishes a direct-trust cohort, provisionally below roughly one hundred and fifty people, from a larger bioregional Scale that may approach five hundred through nested groups. Neither number is settled. The terms and evidence for both levels must be defined before population claims guide governance.
