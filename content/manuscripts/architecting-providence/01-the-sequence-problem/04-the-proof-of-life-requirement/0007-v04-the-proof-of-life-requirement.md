*Before a civilization can scale, it must be real somewhere. The somewhere must be real enough to lose.*

A pilot can be built to attract support or to test a design. Those aims are not identical. Providence needs a test in which participants organize meaningful parts of life around the proposed architecture and can discover what fails under real stakes. A polished demonstration would not meet that requirement.
