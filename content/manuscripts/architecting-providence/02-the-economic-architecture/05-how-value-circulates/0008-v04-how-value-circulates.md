Platform logic lets an operator capture value generated by participants and redistribute part of it on the operator's terms. Protocol logic aims to let value remain with the people and communities that create it.

Providence proposes the second model, but the slogan does not decide fees, ownership, compensation, or the distribution of shared gains. Each mechanism must show who contributes, who benefits, who bears risk, and what the coordinating institution retains.
