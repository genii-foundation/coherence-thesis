Clara Miller's work at the F.B. Heron Foundation and the Omidyar Network's blended-capital model offer contrasting cases of mission-aligned investment. The International Co-operative Alliance, Johnston Birchall, and credit union research examine cooperative capital and governance. Long Finance and the Long-Term Stock Exchange test ways to reward longer horizons. Each example needs current evidence about outcomes, control, and failure before Providence treats it as precedent.
