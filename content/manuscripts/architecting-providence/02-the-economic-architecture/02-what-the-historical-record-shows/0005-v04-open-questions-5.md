Every form of capital carries governance effects. Providence must decide which effects its constitution can accept, then make those decisions enforceable in contracts and law.

Scale remains unresolved. Endowments, cooperatives, public funds, community bonds, and earned revenue each change character as they grow. The project needs staged evidence before treating any combination as adequate.
