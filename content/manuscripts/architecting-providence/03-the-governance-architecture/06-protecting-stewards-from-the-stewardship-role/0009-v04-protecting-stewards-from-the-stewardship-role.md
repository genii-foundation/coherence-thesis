Stewardship can consume the people who perform it. An institution may praise their devotion while depending on unpaid labor, constant availability, or an identity that makes departure feel like failure.

The design needs rotation, succession, supervision, economic support, protected rest, and public accountability. No person or small group should hold a role indefinitely. Stewards need support from people without an interest in preserving them in office.
