*A steward is not a manager with a different title. A steward is someone whose obligation runs to the living system rather than to the metrics used to evaluate it.*

Volume III described stewardship as responsibility to a living system across time. Volume IV asks how stewards are selected, supported, rotated, challenged, and removed. A different title without those mechanisms would change little.
