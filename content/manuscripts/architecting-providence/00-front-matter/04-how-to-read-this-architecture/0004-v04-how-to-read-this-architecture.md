This is closer to a field manual than a river. It offers structural cautions, design requirements, case studies, and research questions. Where it gives an example, the point is to expose a pattern, not prescribe a copy.

The prose sometimes moves from specification into story. That change in register is deliberate. The Cardinal Scale scenarios give the architecture ordinary weather, difficult meetings, awkward succession, and costly choices. They remain design fiction unless the text explicitly says otherwise.
