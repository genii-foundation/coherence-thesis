Within the proposed architecture, Purposeful holds the human development work: mentorship, retreats, gatherings, and a year-long curriculum. Providence holds the coordination infrastructure. Purposeful is the soil. Providence is what might grow from it.

The application is meant to begin modestly. It would help people observe their own state, practice presence, and coordinate with others without surrendering their data or attention. Calling it a biological mentor is an aspiration for the experience, not a verified product claim. The technical, clinical, and ethical limits of that aspiration still require research and public evidence.

The intended path moves from encounter to practice, then from practice to community. People might first meet the work through the books or application, deepen it through gatherings and sustained development, and eventually help form a Scale. The sequence matters because a platform cannot manufacture the culture required to govern it.
