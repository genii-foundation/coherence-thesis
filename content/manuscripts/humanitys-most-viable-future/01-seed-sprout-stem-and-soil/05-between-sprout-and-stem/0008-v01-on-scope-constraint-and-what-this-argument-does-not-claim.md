The sprout was brief by design: five to eight minutes meant to let a pattern already felt, if not yet named, become recognizable.

What follows is not a manifesto, and it asks for no belief, allegiance, or adoption. Its scope is narrower, and for that reason more demanding.


This argument concerns constraint. It asks a single question:

*What must be true, biologically, relationally, and systemically, for any complex civilization to remain coherent as its power scales?*

Everything that follows is an exploration of that condition, and nothing more. Before structure can be examined, the boundaries of the claim must be drawn, so that what is being said is not mistaken for what is not.
