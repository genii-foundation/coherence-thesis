## Independent conclusion

The baseline and current source were compared proposition by proposition. No accidental reversal, missing canonical Initiate, missing Tenet, or unmarked increase in certainty was found. The largest changes are necessary changes of modality: numerology becomes symbolism, a universal sequence becomes a working map, physiological proof becomes a research question, and private mentor judgment becomes accountable practice bounded by consent. The source now states safeguarding requirements. It does not establish that any existing program meets them, and publication must preserve that distinction.
