- declarations about what the reader has felt, carried, wanted, remembered, or was made to become;
- claims that each earlier volume became more transcendent;
- claims that Volume IV proved the architecture buildable;
- physiological signals treated as moral or social truth;
- the roughly 150 population as a proven natural limit;
- planetary consciousness presented as demonstrated fact;
- duplicate title matter and malformed quotation emphasis;
- repeated summaries that explained the same nest after it was already whole.
