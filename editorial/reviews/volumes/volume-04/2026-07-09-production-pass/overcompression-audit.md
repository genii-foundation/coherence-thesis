## Why the major cuts are defensible

The baseline repeated most chapters through a coda, a second coda movement, ceremonial recap, research superlative, and automatic visionary landing. Much of that repetition made speculative scenes sound reported, turned a heading into proof, or assigned belief and participation to the reader. The current manuscript retains the proposition, mechanism, qualification, counterargument, and strongest image once. It also adds legal, ethical, privacy, labor, disability, consent, and nonparticipant safeguards that the baseline often omitted.

The reduction is still editorially high. Defensibility is not approval. The author must decide whether the faster architecture retains the desired intellectual amplitude, especially in stewardship, culture, development, contact, invitation, donor, adaptive-power, first-Scale, and dragon chapters.
