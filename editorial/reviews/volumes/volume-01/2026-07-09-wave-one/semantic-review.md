## Unresolved queries

### 1. What moral claim does the Seed make about historical extraction?

- Baseline, former line 182: `The Seed does not claim that extraction was wrong.`
- Current line 138: `The Seed does not claim that extraction was always ineffective.`
- Concern: this replaces a moral proposition with an effectiveness proposition. It appears to reconcile the former sentence with the manuscript's separate assertion that extraction is unjust, but doing so silently invents the reconciliation.
- Author decision needed: retain moral bracketing, state that extraction was unjust but conditionally effective, or adopt the effectiveness-only formulation and make the moral position explicit elsewhere.

### 2. Is coherence the direction of living development, or is that only a possibility?

- Baseline, former line 529: coherence `is, in some real sense, the direction in which living systems become more themselves`.
- Current line 404: coherence `may be the direction in which living systems become more fully themselves`.
- Concern: the revision changes an asserted metaphysical inference into speculation. The change may be philosophically prudent, but it changes modality and the force of the Soil's conclusion.
- Author decision needed: approve the speculative formulation or restore the asserted inference.

### 3. Is COHERENCE the relation itself or the portable record of the relation?

- Current line 549 says COHERENCE is not a token for coherence and is the coherence made between people.
- The same line says its record points back to the relation that made it.
- Current lines 539 and 551 describe a memory system and an accumulable unit.
- Concern: the passage closes the gap between sign and thing, then reintroduces a record that represents the thing. The Volume I voice card names this exact ontology as an open author decision. The master ledger defines the Currency of Presence as a memory system and COHERENCE as its unit, but it does not resolve whether the unit is identical with the relation.
- Author decision needed: define COHERENCE as the relation itself with a separate portable attestation, define it as the record of that relation, or supply another explicit ontology. The name, memory, and accumulation paragraphs must then use one model consistently.

### 4. What does the Maren vignette say the currency records?

- Current line 527 names Bio-Consensus as the primary minting path.
- Current line 535 says measurable shifts are attested by others and distinguishes the system from self-report.
- Current line 583 says the currency records the other participants' testimony.
- Concern: `testimony` can mean a subjective report, while the canonical architecture says the primary path is biometric and local. The vignette can therefore be read as contradicting the mechanism and its anti-counterfeiting argument.
- Author decision needed: specify whether the vignette refers to a biometric attestation, human testimony, Proof of Council as an ancillary path, or a reviewed combination. Do not infer the mechanism in prose before this is decided.

### 5. Does coherence govern technological power, or might it merely govern it?

- Baseline, former line 816: civilization's future `will not be decided by how much intelligence we build, but by whether we become coherent enough to wield it`.
- Current line 633: the future depends on both how much intelligence is built and whether civilization becomes coherent enough to wield it, and `The second condition may govern the first.`
- Concern: this changes the volume's concluding doctrine from coherence as the governing constraint to coherence as a possible cofactor. It conflicts with the Seed's claim that coherence is a constraint the design must satisfy and weakens the book's final recognition.
- Author decision needed: restore coherence as the governing constraint, or approve a two-factor thesis and revise the Orientation, Seed, Stem, and protected conclusion so they agree.
