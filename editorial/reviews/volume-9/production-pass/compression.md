## What was compressed

- The number-nine exposition retained symbolic meaning and lost numerology as proof.
- The recap retained all eight prior volumes under their canonical titles and removed duplicate crescendos.
- The architecture stack lost claims that mechanisms, sites, records, privacy, and governance already work.
- Entelechy, Coherent Resonance, Land-NFT, SDAC, Container, Proof of Council, and the Cardinal Scale were separated from claims their definitions cannot yet support.
- The solved-versus-unsolved section became commitments and open problems.
- Recruitment became verification, bounded pilots, safeguards, and a public record.
- The closing lost the unfulfilled live-network promise and generic reader recognition while preserving water, nest, stone, seed, weather, shade, fruit, and roots.

## Overcompression review

The 40.7 percent reduction remains within a strong Level 2 and selective Level 3 pass. The recap carried the highest duplication, while the land, water, material floor, human matching, care figures, and closing images were retained or restored. All five numbered sections remain in order.

Author approval is required because the revision changes the volume from recruitment around a purported operating Scale into a builder's brief for an unverified proposal. That change preserves the manuscript's honest uncertainty but materially alters its publication posture.

## Why the reduction remains defensible

Volume IX is strongest when it performs one task: return the series to a bounded place and name what a builder must prove. Repeated claims that the spiral closes, the first ground exists, and the sequence is settled weakened that task because the same manuscript then listed fundamental unknowns. The current text lets uncertainty become part of the architecture.
