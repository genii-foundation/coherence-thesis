I compared the baseline and revision through the full volume, then reread the revised sequence for breath, image, register, repetition, humor, and emotional movement. I treated clarity gains as gains only when they preserved the volume's characteristic alternation between compact propositions and cumulative reasoning.

The incoming revision reduced the Soil and Flower from 5,293 to 3,891 raw Markdown words, a reduction of 26.5 percent. That degree of compression was not itself a defect. Most cuts removed repeated caveats, meta commentary, false contrasts, or explanations the image already carried. The corrections in this review bring those movements to 3,936 words, still 25.6 percent below the baseline.

## Findings and corrections

### Opening and Sprout

The new opening is cleaner and more credible than the baseline. It removes drafting notes, ceremonial pacing claims, and repeated assurances without losing the developmental form. One sentence repeated "work" until the invitation sounded procedural, so I recast it to invite capable readers to help build what the papers outline.

The Sprout's turn from cultural practice to measurable coherence had been compressed into one dense paragraph. The sentence "That assumption is now failing" no longer had room to function as a hinge. I restored paragraph breaks around it. No discarded Sprout passage needed wholesale restoration.

### Soil

The Soil survived the compression conceptually, but several of its best movements had been made merely smooth.

- The flame image had become explanatory prose. I restored the more exact comparison: intelligence is less like a substance than a flame, and it requires conditions to burn.
- "Being known, reflected, recognized, and answered" weakened both grammar and intimacy. I restored the relational sequence "being known: reflected, recognized, and responded to."
- "A kind of reverence arrives through noticing rather than demand" flattened a deliberate three-beat turn. I restored "not demanded, not believed, but noticed" with a colon, preserving the zero prohibited punctuation rule.
- The revision removed "more alive" from the paragraph that defines reverence through observation. I restored the image and let adaptive capacity, creativity, and repair specify it.
- "More fully themselves" normalized a stranger and better phrase. I restored "more themselves."
- The Seed's return at depth had been compressed from a two-step recognition into a label. I restored the pause between "not a preference" and "It is a prerequisite."

These changes restore the descent and return without reinstating the baseline's repeated explanation of why the book exists.

### Flower, PURPOSEFUL, and the Currency of Presence

The Flower's large cuts are mostly justified. Providence is easier to distinguish from PURPOSEFUL, and the distinction between infrastructure that carries trust and relationships that grow trust remains alive. The line "The need is vast. The attempt is small, and knows it" survives intact.

The protected sentence "It is wisdom exchanged for future" had been joined to its setup in a single paragraph. I restored its isolation. The line needs silence after it.

The Currency section retains its useful strangeness around presence and presencing while removing several overclaims about the token and the relation being identical. I corrected one dropped preposition in "shifts that others attest to" and restored some human cadence to the sentence about accumulation and becoming whole. I did not restore the stronger identity claim, because that would exceed literary authority and could alter the architecture's meaning.

### Surveillance safeguards

Pass. The compression preserves all four inversions of social credit: voluntary entry, bounded scope, self-sovereign local records, and an unconditional floor of belonging. It also preserves raw signal locality, thin attestations, revocability, open inspection, and the right to fork. The section remains steady rather than soothing, and the lines about the ceiling, floor, cage, and tool retain their force. No literary correction was needed.

### Maren's room

The incoming revision kept the plot but converted some of the scene's warm, slightly odd language into administrative phrasing. I made four corrections.

- I restored the world's lack of a "slot" for Maren's capacity. "Place" was cleaner and less alive.
- I restored the contrast between entering through relationship and making an account.
- I replaced the bureaucratic movement from ability to attestation with people who are steadied and "say so," while preserving that the currency records their testimony rather than Maren's self-claim.
- I restored the network doing the one thing Maren could never do for herself, then letting her earned trust travel.

The room now has an emotional arc again: an unseen capacity, self-doubt, recognition by others, trust that travels, and a frightened room brought back to itself.

### Scales and burden of proof

The cell, surface, body, and connective tissue sequence remains coherent. The revision had replaced the strange line about there being no thing in charge with the more conventional "no central authority." I restored the oddity while retaining the word "central" for precision. The result keeps both the institutional claim and the voice.

The burden of proof section had lost the cathedral image. I restored the instruction not to build the cathedral and hope, then to test the foundation before the weight goes on it. The image gives the falsifiability sequence a human memory without weakening its seriousness.

### Closing invitation

The incoming Invitation fell from 670 to 358 raw Markdown words. The role list became clearer, but the opening and final movement had lost the volume's downward and upward geometry. I restored the following:

- the sense of having gone the whole way down and most of the way back up
- the two-beat distinction between a conclusion and an opening
- a more human statement of the relation between building intelligence and becoming coherent enough to wield it
- "The future is not yet written" as the setup for our already writing it
- the ritual sequence "to do it knowingly," "to help cultivate the substrate," and "to tend the soil"

The invitation now returns to the seed and plant structure rather than ending as a compressed recruitment summary. The final protected line remains exact.

## Unresolved authorial questions

1. Does the heading "Four Movements" refer only to Seed, Sprout, Stem, and Soil? The passage beneath it also introduces the Flower. The current structure can be defended because the Flower begins Part Two, but the heading may make a careful reader count five and wonder whether one leaf has escaped the botany department.

2. The baseline called the Currency of Presence "a way for the realest things about us to stop being invisible." The revision says it "gives unseen human capacities a way to become legible." The revision is more exact, but "realest things" is stranger and more intimate. I left the precise version because restoring the older line is a matter of authorial taste rather than clear correction.

Neither question blocks the literary pass. Both should be resolved by the author rather than by further anonymous smoothing.
