The complete catalog in `editorial-standards.md` was applied to the whole revised source. Automated findings were treated as prompts. This is the lead review record. The completed fresh independent slop review is recorded below.

| Catalog category | Result | Evidence and action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 4.1 Throat clearing | corrected | Removed openings that announced importance, depth, or the chapter's task before beginning the thought. Kept only necessary wayfinding in a long volume. |
| 4.2 Generic transitions | corrected | Deleted ceremonial `and yet`, `all of which`, `which points`, and conclusion scaffolds where the relation was already visible. Named cause, limit, or sequence when needed. |
| 4.3 False antithesis | corrected | Removed repeated `not X but Y` constructions and false choices between institutions and people, trust and safeguards, success and contribution, system and relationship. Bounded contrasts remain where both sides are real. |
| 4.4 Synthetic symmetry | corrected | Reduced triadic example chains, mirrored chapter endings, and repeated developmental recaps. The PURPOSEFUL acronym and selected parallel sequences remain intentional. |
| 4.5 Abstract noun clusters | corrected | Recovered actors, actions, conditions, and consequences in purpose, trust, stewardship, and coordination passages. Canonical abstractions remain where they name distinct concepts. |
| 4.6 Inflated significance | corrected | Removed largest, rarest, greatest, most consequential, defining challenge, and proof language unless the text earned a bounded use. |
| 4.7 Vague grandeur | corrected | Replaced species destiny, unprecedented capability, and generic future-of-humanity claims with concrete programs, relationships, risks, and decisions. |
| 4.8 Redundant restatement | corrected | Removed repeated chapter conclusions and dozens of recurrences of potential becoming purpose, contribution, trust, collaboration, and stewardship. Kept the sequence where it performs real structural work. |
| 4.9 Repetitive scaffolds | corrected | Varied or removed `This is`, `There is`, `The question is`, and `Every civilization` openings. Display matter remains intentionally repetitive. |
| 4.10 Vague reference | corrected | Named Purposeful, Providence, program status, Cardinal Scale, intention signals, or the relevant institution instead of distant `this`, `it`, and `they`. |
| 4.11 Conceptual laundering | corrected | Recognition, purpose, trust, and stewardship are separated from evidence for their consequences. Naming the term no longer serves as proof. |
| 4.12 Causal overreach | corrected | Changed universal and mechanical claims to can, may, tends, supports, or requires testing. Preserved causal language only for direct structural relations. |
| 4.13 Faux precision | corrected | Removed faux arithmetic about eight billion lives and the unsupported ten-thousand-garment count. Marked population figures and implementation status as unresolved. |
| 4.14 Sterile hedging | corrected | Qualifications now name the uncertainty: historical variation, program status, measurement, population, memory, or metaphysical modality. No layers of empty `may` were added. |
| 4.15 Generic uplift | corrected | Removed automatic crescendos from chapter endings and changed the final command into a free invitation. Hope now arrives through Donna, work, stewardship, and possible communities. |
| 4.16 Performed intimacy | corrected | The reader is no longer told what they feel, carry, need, or were born to do. Donna's recognition does not become authority over the reader. |
| 4.17 Dramatic fragments | corrected | Rejoined simulated revelations into argument. Title matter, chapter labels, the Donna question, and a few earned landings remain intentional. |
| 4.18 Overexplained metaphor | corrected | Let cathedral, seed, garden, forest, room, dragon, and pinned hem carry meaning without several abstract translations. Removed competing metaphors from the same movement. |
| 4.19 Mechanical cadence | corrected | Shortened long clause stacks, varied paragraph length, and removed the repeated long-exposition then tiny-verdict rhythm. |
| 4.20 Lexical monoculture | corrected | Reduced unnecessary recurrence of civilization, architecture, potential, purpose, contribution, coordination, and stewardship. Retained each when it names the exact canonical concept. |
| 4.21 Empty intensifiers | corrected | Removed deeply, truly, genuinely, extraordinarily, remarkably, and similar modifiers where they supplied no evidence. |
| 4.22 Exhaustive pairings | corrected | Reduced formulaic `both`, `whether`, and `from X to Y` sequences. Retained bounded pairs such as Scale singular and ICONS plural. |
| 4.23 Meta claims about the text | intentional | Most ceremonial references to the volume were removed. Remaining volume navigation identifies cross-volume sequence, implementation posture, or the Donna return and is necessary wayfinding. |
| 4.24 Gloss without substance | corrected | Removed sentences whose only work was grandeur, inevitability, or uplift. Every retained high-register sentence carries a proposition, image, question, or emotional movement. |
