## Debt

The master ledger says future volumes will become accessible through The Coherence Thesis Network, which it describes as currently being built. The repository does not yet point readers to a defined network surface or status record.

## Evidence

The promise appears in the ledger's opening scope note and governs how readers are told the outward-facing nine-volume series will continue. Volumes VII and IX also describe future books, network functions, or participation without a delivery contract or linked status surface.

## Paydown criteria

Publish and link the promised network surface with an honest current status, or revise the ledger so it does not claim that a specific access path is being built before that commitment exists.
