## Debt

Volumes II, IV, and IX invite builders, funders, communities, researchers, and possible participants to act, but the manuscripts and standalone exports provide no consistent link, contact path, repository, eligibility account, or durable next action.

## Evidence

Volume II says there are ways to learn more. Volume IV tells readers the work awaits them. Volume IX invites participation around real places and projects. The invitations cannot be acted on reliably from the reader or PDF.

## Paydown criteria

Add one durable public participation route that survives reader, Markdown, and PDF publication. State the current opportunities, eligibility, consent conditions, ownership, responsible contact, and response expectations. Verify that the route works, or remove claims that an access path already exists.
