## Debt

Volumes I, II, and VIII called Bio-Consensus the Currency of Presence, while the master ledger defines Bio-Consensus as the primary biometric minting path and COHERENCE as the currency unit. The first-wave corrections paid down the problem in Volumes I and II, but the corpus-level conflation remains in Volume VIII. Volumes III and IX also need to keep the proposed biometric path, Proof of Council, record, and money functions separate when discussing validation.

## Evidence

The wording collapsed mechanism, process, and unit into one term and contradicted the canon reference. Volume VIII still calls "Bio-Consensus" the Currency of Presence in its summary of Providence.

## Paydown criteria

Name the Currency of Presence, COHERENCE, and Bio-Consensus as distinct concepts in every volume, verify the definitions against the master ledger, and run a corpus-wide terminology search before resolving the item again.

- 2026-07-09: Found during the integrator close read and returned to the Volume I lead editor.
- 2026-07-09: Corrected in Volume I and checked against the master ledger.

## Partial paydown

Volumes I and II now identify COHERENCE as the currency unit and Bio-Consensus as the primary biometric path by which it is intended to be minted. Volume VIII still requires correction and independent review.
