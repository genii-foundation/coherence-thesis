## Debt

Volume II promises to make coherence measurable across nervous systems, institutions, ecologies, and the relationship between intelligence and life, but later chapters offer indicators and examples rather than an operational method across all four domains.

## Evidence

The promise appears near current line 127. The relevant material does not define measures, boundaries, validation, or how the four levels relate. The four-channel physiological passage can also read as a validated taxonomy even though the source admits that trauma, contemplative practice, empathy, spoofing, and physiological diversity remain unresolved.

## Paydown criteria

Add a bounded cross-domain measurement framework with operational definitions, falsifiability, evidentiary limits, spoofing analysis, and explicit relationships among levels, or narrow the opening promise so it examines what measurement would require. Obtain disability, neurodiversity, trauma, and biometric-ethics review before treating the four-channel image as a model.
