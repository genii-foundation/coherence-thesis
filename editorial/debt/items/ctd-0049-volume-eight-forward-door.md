## Debt

The final source note in Volume VIII says its "forward door" remains set to Volume VII, even though Volume VII precedes it and Volume IX is the next volume.

## Evidence

The note may intend a thematic return to the Seven Initiates rather than chronological navigation. Calling that movement forward makes the series direction ambiguous and reads like an internal editorial instruction left in the book.

## Paydown criteria

Decide whether the note points backward thematically or forward to Volume IX. Rewrite it as reader-facing continuity, or remove it if it is production metadata rather than part of the published work.

- 2026-07-09: Recorded during the first corpus-wide promise and navigation scan.
