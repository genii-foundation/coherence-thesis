## Debt

Volume VII asks readers to doubt the work, then appears to ask them to make it harder to understand. The sentence may contain the opposite of the intended word and conflicts with the corpus clarity standard.

## Evidence

Current lines 21 to 31 say that if the work is not found inscrutable, the reader should take it as a charge to make it so.

## Paydown criteria

Confirm the intended word and philosophical posture. Revise the invocation so doubt increases clarity and challenge rather than obscurity. Read the complete opening aloud and record author approval.
