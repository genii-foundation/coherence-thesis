## Debt

Volume III's title and subtitle promise a construction account for infrastructure civilization needs, while the body correctly describes Providence as one fallible, unvalidated research and governance proposal.

## Evidence

The tension runs from the title matter through the preface, evidence tiers, roadmap, imagined clock precision, epilogue, and appendix. Readers cannot tell whether the work is philosophy, an institutional prospectus, a research agenda, design fiction, or a build manual. High-register part titles such as `The Reckoning`, `The Living Reality`, `The Long Now`, and `The Invitation` may also carry a different posture from the newly sober prose.

## Paydown criteria

Choose the publication category and desired degree of prescription. Decide which exact times, counts, outcomes, and display titles belong to acknowledged design fiction. Align the title, subtitle, preface, section labels, claim force, scholarly apparatus, and reader invitation with that posture, then record explicit author approval.
