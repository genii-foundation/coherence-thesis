## Debt

Volumes VII and IX treat internal numerical and planetary symmetry as evidence for the thesis. Symbolic architecture may carry literary or spiritual meaning, but repetition inside the work does not establish truth outside it.

## Evidence

Volume VII lines 97 to 111 assign planets and qualities to volume numbers, call seven the measure of complete unfolding, and say the hidden order is evidence because surface and depth agree. Volume IX line 31 adds digital-root arithmetic as the meaning of nine and uses it to explain the Cardinal Scale. The master ledger lines 85 to 101 record the scheme as canon.

## Paydown criteria

Decide whether the number and planet scheme is private symbolism, literary architecture, historical claim, or metaphysical canon. Remove evidentiary language unless independent support exists. Fact-check every historical, mathematical, and astronomical assertion and mark symbolic interpretation as interpretation.
