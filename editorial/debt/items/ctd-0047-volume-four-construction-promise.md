## Debt

Volume III promises that the next volume turns from principle toward construction. Volume IV supplies extensive specifications but still defers parts of the non-gatekeeping, trust, measurement, and institutional mechanism to later work.

## Evidence

The transition near the end of Volume III reads as a categorical handoff. Volume IV later identifies central technical and governance functions that remain unresolved or must be learned only through building.

## Paydown criteria

Inventory every mechanism the handoff promises, identify the exact Volume IV fulfillment or continuing uncertainty, then narrow the promise or complete the missing construction account without presenting design fiction as implementation evidence.

- 2026-07-09: Raised by the independent Volume III semantic review.
- 2026-07-09: Expanded after the full corpus audit required every promised construction area to be mapped to an exact Volume IV section or explicit continuing uncertainty.
