## Debt

Volume I commits the project to test physiological detectability first, under adversarial and independent scrutiny, publish what the instruments can and cannot see, and build only on what survives. No protocol, registration, result, or public research route is linked.

## Evidence

The commitment appears in Measurement and Falsifiability and returns in Boundaries and Burden of Proof. Volume III still carries an evidence queue, an unvalidated Bio-Consensus mechanism, experimental pulse and endocrine claims, and a three-to-five-year planning horizon rather than a completed protocol.

## Paydown criteria

Publish a reviewable protocol, methods, data constraints, attack and abuse model, independent adversarial review, and results, with participant protections, disability and cultural review, milestones, dependencies, and stopping rules. Otherwise recast the passage and planning horizon as proposed research norms rather than work already under way.
