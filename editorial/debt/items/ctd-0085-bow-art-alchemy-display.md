## Debt

The named forms B.O.W., Body of Worship, BOW Councils, and Art & Alchemy vary across titles, prose, tables, and the master ledger without an approved display rule.

## Evidence

Volume VI line 341 and Volume VIII lines 310 and 405 use BOW. Volume VII line 11 uses `B.O.W :`, line 226 uses `Art & Alchemy`, line 358 uses `Art / Alchemy`, and line 407 uses BOW Councils. Volume IX line 53 uses BOW and lowercase art and alchemy. The master ledger lines 52 and 76 use B.O.W. and `Art & Alchemy`.

## Paydown criteria

Approve canonical display and spoken forms for B.O.W., Body of Worship, B.O.W. Councils, and Art & Alchemy. Apply them to headings, tables, prose, metadata, and the master ledger. Preserve the ampersand where the approved display identity requires it.

## History

- 2026-07-09: Recorded by the initial independent corpus audit.
- 2026-07-10: Standardized current production prose to the master-ledger forms while retaining the author query about final display and spoken identity.

Councils` for the proposed body. The current master ledger also uses `B.O.W.` and `Art & Alchemy`. `editorial/reviews/volumes/volume-06/2026-07-09-production-pass/semantic-review.md` and `editorial/reviews/volumes/volume-07/2026-07-09-production-pass/semantic-review.md` preserve the naming question rather than inventing approval. The item remains a query until the author approves the canonical written and spoken forms and the decision is applied to every title, table, metadata record, and ledger entry.
