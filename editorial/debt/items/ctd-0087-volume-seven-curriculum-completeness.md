## Debt

Volume VII presents a fifty-two-week curriculum and institutional pathway as an architecture, but it does not specify enough content, learning design, facilitation, assessment, or safety structure to test or deliver the promise.

## Evidence

Current lines 343 to 369 promise fifty-two weekly initiations and a year-long path. The only worked example is touch across seven Initiates at lines 351 to 363. Lines 399 to 415 add councils, an Academy of Muses, a Circle of Restoration, Four Chapters, and a Twelve-Year Trajectory without operational definitions.

## Paydown criteria

Supply the complete curriculum, learning objectives, facilitator standards, contraindications, assessment approach, and safety apparatus, or reframe the volume as a conceptual sketch. Define every named body and trajectory or retire the orphan term.
