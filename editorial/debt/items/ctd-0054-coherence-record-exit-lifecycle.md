## Debt

Volume I says the coherence record dissolves when a participant leaves, then says a participant may leave while carrying or destroying the record. Both cannot describe one record lifecycle without another distinction.

## Evidence

The contradiction appears within the four social-credit safeguards and the invariant summary. Later volumes add portable records, selective disclosure, recovery, revocation, deletion, and durable standing without one canonical lifecycle or one tested right-to-leave procedure.

## Paydown criteria

Define enrollment, withdrawal, export, portability, recovery, revocation, deletion, retained attestations, third-party copies, coercion cases, and alias or key invalidation. Use distinct terms if a participation record, personal archive, credential, wallet, and public attestation behave differently.
