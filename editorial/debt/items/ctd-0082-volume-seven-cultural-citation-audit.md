## Debt

Volume VII claims authority from Latin, Greek, Taoist, cosmological, and other cultural traditions without a source register or enough distinction between historical account and the manuscript's own synthesis.

## Evidence

Current lines 135 to 153 associate Gaia with intelligence in the between, give a Latin account of genius and genii, and attribute the Four Windows of Perception to Taoist philosophy. Lines 441 to 459 closely echo language associated with Kahlil Gibran without attribution. The volume contains no complete source register.

## Paydown criteria

Verify primary texts, translations, etymology, and tradition-specific interpretations. Attribute adaptations clearly. Invite qualified review for Taoist and other living-tradition claims. Add a stable source apparatus that preserves the difference between inherited teaching and author synthesis.
