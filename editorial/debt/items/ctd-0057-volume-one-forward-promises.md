## Debt

Volume I promises later treatment of artificial intelligence, engineering, governance, economics, Bio-Consensus, and the first Scale, then offers routes for scientists, builders, communities, and funders. The first editorial pass has not yet proven that every handoff reaches the promised material or an actionable route.

## Evidence

The promises are concentrated in the Stem, Flower, and Invitation. Several later volumes discuss the topics, but open construction, access, measurement, governance, and collaborator-route debt shows that topic recurrence is not the same as fulfillment.

## Paydown criteria

Create a forward-promise ledger with one destination and fulfillment test per promise. Verify that the destination exists and delivers the claimed depth, and provide durable participation routes wherever Volume I asks the reader to act.
