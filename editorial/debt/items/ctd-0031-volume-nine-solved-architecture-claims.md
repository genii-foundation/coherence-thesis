## Debt

Volume IX declares that the unit is sound and the sequence is settled while listing the currency, legal vessel, capital structure, adversarial security, measurement, initiation, and council scaling as fundamental unsolved problems.

## Evidence

The claims appear within the same "What We Know, and What We Do Not" section. Several unknowns determine whether the proposed unit can exist safely, lawfully, or at all, so calling the architecture settled overstates the manuscript's own account. Revised Volume III provides the controlling counterexample by separating proposal, evidence, implementation, and stopping conditions rather than treating narrated detail as proof.

## Paydown criteria

Separate tested findings, design commitments, hypotheses, and unresolved dependencies. Retain certainty only where evidence or a genuine invariant supports it, and align the closing invitation with the resulting implementation status.
