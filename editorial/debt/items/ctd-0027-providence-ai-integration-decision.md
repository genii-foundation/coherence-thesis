## Debt

Volume II promises that Providence's unresolved AI integration design will be developed openly with builders, but the corpus does not yet define the decision process, governing criteria, or current outcome.

## Evidence

The promise appears in both the Providence and Artificial Intelligence chapter and the later Reference Model. The proposed system would handle values, intentions, trust signals, community alignment, and deliberation, so the unresolved choice has direct privacy, power, and governance consequences.

## Paydown criteria

Define the public decision process, named governance authority, model limits, nonnegotiable safeguards, and forbidden uses. State the current product posture, remove redundant promises, provide a real site or repository decision surface, and link the manuscript claim to a durable decision record when one exists.
