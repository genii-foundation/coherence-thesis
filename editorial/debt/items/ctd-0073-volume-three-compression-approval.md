## Debt

Volume III has undergone developmental compression far beyond routine line editing. The revision is clearer and more credible, and literary review passed it, but the scale of removal changes voice, pacing, warmth, and argumentative connective tissue enough to require explicit author approval before publication.

## Evidence

The slop review withholds publication approval pending an author read-aloud decision at the Douro opening, current lines 89 to 93; the Seven Initiates, lines 255 to 275; the governance sketch, lines 846 to 905; and the invitation and financing sequence, lines 1230 to 1350. ## Paydown criteria

Compare the immutable baseline and current revision aloud at every control passage. Record whether the intervention is approved, too light, or too strong. Restore only unique images, premises, transitions, or breath patterns whose absence is felt, without reviving unsupported certainty, generic uplift, or repeated scaffolds. Update the voice card, human review record, sentence ledger, and structure ledger with the decision, then rerun semantic, literary, and complete slop review.
