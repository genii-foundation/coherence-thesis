## Debt

Volume I silently replaces the baseline statement that the Seed does not claim extraction was wrong with the different statement that extraction was not always ineffective.

## Evidence

The replacement shifts from moral bracketing to historical effectiveness. Elsewhere the volume calls extraction unjust, so the change appears to reconcile a contradiction without an author decision.

## Paydown criteria

Choose whether the Seed brackets moral judgment, states that extraction was unjust but conditionally effective, or adopts an effectiveness-only formulation with the moral position made explicit elsewhere. Apply the decision consistently.
