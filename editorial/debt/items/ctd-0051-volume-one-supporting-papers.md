## Debt

The Opening says many extensive papers exist and carry the biological, economic, governance, technological, and implementation rigor behind the book, but it provides no inventory, citation, repository, or reader route to those papers.

## Evidence

Current lines 35 to 45 use the supporting corpus to establish scholarly and technical seriousness. A reader cannot inspect that support from the manuscript or site.

## Paydown criteria

Inventory the papers, verify that they support the claims attributed to them, publish or link stable versions, and attach each load-bearing manuscript claim to its actual supporting work. Narrow the assertion if the corpus is incomplete or inaccessible.
