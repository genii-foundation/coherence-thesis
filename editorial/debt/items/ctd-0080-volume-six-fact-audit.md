## Debt

Volume VI compresses scientific, historical, social, and metaphysical claims into lyrical declarations without a source apparatus or consistent evidentiary modality.

## Evidence

Current lines 123 to 127 present coherence as the grain of reality and extraction as a false current. Lines 271 to 279 say body signals are becoming measurable enough to support currency. Lines 457 to 459 assert a natural community limit of roughly one hundred and fifty.

## Paydown criteria

Classify each material claim as evidence, inference, metaphor, or author conviction. Source bounded factual claims, preserve the lyrical register where it is clearly image rather than proof, and revise universals that cannot be earned. Align population claims with CTD-0036 and measurement claims with CTD-0009.
