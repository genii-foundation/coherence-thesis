## Debt

The manuscripts use phrases such as "for the first time in history" and make universal claims about every civilization, all governance, centuries of extraction, and defining challenges without a documented historical review.

## Evidence

Volume II still uses first-in-history, every-civilization, all-governance, centuries-of-extraction, and defining-century claims. Several make coherence true by definition rather than by comparative evidence. The claims carry more force than the current citations establish and are not necessary to preserve the core proposal.

## Paydown criteria

Complete a defensible historical review and cite it, or replace each universal with a bounded claim that names the period, region, mechanism, comparison class, and known exceptions.
