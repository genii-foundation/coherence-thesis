## Debt

Volume IV's repeated coda system creates a second narrative and argumentative layer. Some codas merely restate the chapter, while at least one reverses its outcome and others change speculative scenes into apparent fact.

## Evidence

Volume IV promises a coda after nearly every chapter at lines 83 and 109 and contains 28 Coda: In Plain Terms units. The donor chapter says the gift is lost at lines 1427 to 1435, while its coda says the gift was accepted at line 1454. Line 1588 repeats the Cardinal Scale name and converts a proposed community into a real one.

## Paydown criteria

Give every coda one disposition: retain, integrate, rewrite, or remove. Verify every retained coda against the chapter's claims, chronology, modality, and tense. Remove the formula wherever it adds no distinct reader value. Complete a boundary read across each chapter, coda, and following section.

## History

- 2026-07-09: Recorded by the initial independent corpus audit.
- 2026-07-10: Resolved after all twenty-eight codas received an explicit remove-and-integrate disposition and fresh review found no missing image, mechanism, or claim requiring restoration.
- 2026-07-11: Reopened during the pull request split because all twenty-eight codas remain in the current Volume IV source.

## Prior paydown

The unmerged Volume IV revision retired the repeated coda system after reviewing each unit. That work remains proposed. Historical coda routes also require explicit continuity before the source change can close this debt.
