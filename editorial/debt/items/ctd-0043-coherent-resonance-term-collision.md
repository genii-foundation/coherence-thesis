## Debt

Volume VII defines Coherent Resonance as a quality of being and a steady relational state. Volume IX reuses the same proper noun for a self-owned wallet that stores a portable record of accumulated coherence.

## Evidence

The Volume VII curriculum treats Coherent Resonance as an internal technology learned through practice. Volume IX says COHERENCE gathers into a wallet called Coherent Resonance. Readers cannot tell whether the wallet records the state, is the state, or has borrowed its name without a canon decision.

## Paydown criteria

Give the inner quality and the technical record distinct names unless their identity is intentional and defensible. Define the relation among state, evidence, record, wallet, and currency, then align every occurrence and the master ledger.
