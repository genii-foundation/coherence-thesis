## Debt

Volume VIII moves from a witnessed suicide and a country-club conversation to a causal explanation for ecological catastrophe. The scenes may establish moral and emotional orientation, but they cannot prove the planetary causal claim stated.

## Evidence

Current lines 14 to 40 narrate the two scenes, then say ecological catastrophe is downstream of a catastrophe of the heart and that birds were lost because people learned to treat vanishing as spectacle.

## Paydown criteria

Decide what the scenes can honestly bear. Separate witness, interpretation, analogy, and causal claim. Review the suicide scene for necessity, dignity, content warning, and potential harm. Obtain trauma-sensitive editorial review before author approval.
