## Debt

The revised volume often moves through compact proposition, qualification, list, and verdict. Five consecutive governance and sovereignty chapters add a dense legal register through repeated `should`, `must`, and `cannot` constructions. The distinctions and many taxonomies are necessary, but the repeated pulse can make unlike sections sound administratively even.

## Evidence

The final slop review counted 348 triad candidates across 20,087 words, with concentrations in institutional inheritance, the Seven Initiates, scientific requirements, governance, safeguards, ecological constraints, and financing. The literary review found no safe broad correction before the underlying positions receive author approval.

## Paydown criteria

After the constitutional and authorial decisions are approved, perform one complete read-aloud cadence pass. Mark every functional taxonomy. Recast only synthetic lists and redundant modal scaffolds. Preserve every right, prohibition, qualification, evidence class, and distinction between proposal and binding limit.
