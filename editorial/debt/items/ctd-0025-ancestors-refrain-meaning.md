## Debt

The recurring phrase "the ancestors are watching" shifts among literal spiritual presence, metaphorical inheritance, and the judgment of future generations.

## Evidence

The refrain appears in continuity material and the epilogue. The lead revision offers a two-direction intergenerational reading, but that interpretation has not received author approval and may narrow an intended theology.

## Paydown criteria

Choose the refrain's literal, metaphorical, theological, or intergenerational meaning. Revise each occurrence so it carries that meaning consistently without explaining away its poetic force, then record author approval.
