## Debt

The revised Earth Compact is much shorter and less inhabited than the human, technical, economic, and governance architecture around it. That restraint may honestly reflect the missing evidence, or it may leave ecological accountability feeling appended rather than lived.

## Evidence

The section preserves land as more than stage dressing and removes unsupported energy claims, but current lines 1163 to 1218 provide little concrete place, practice, or consequence compared with the surrounding parts.

## Paydown criteria

Choose whether the compact remains a concise constitutional constraint or gains one concrete, sourced place or scene. Define ecological system boundaries, baselines, adverse outcomes, and audit methods under CTD-0009. Any addition must advance ecological accountability and material consequence, not generic nature lyricism or unsupported benefit claims.
