## Debt

Volume I describes three live Doors, a year-long curriculum, hand-matched mentors, embodied gatherings, and future application reach to anyone ready to enter. The corpus does not provide a consistent operational status, evidence of delivery, or public access path.

## Evidence

The Flower moves between present-tense program description and future application integration. Volumes III, V, VII, and IX elaborate mentors, Doors, gatherings, curricula, councils, academies, and delivery pathways, but the reader cannot tell which offerings exist, are piloting, are designed, or remain aspirational.

## Paydown criteria

Audit each program and feature, publish its current status and access path, document evidence and participant protections where it operates, and revise every manuscript claim to distinguish present operation from roadmap.
