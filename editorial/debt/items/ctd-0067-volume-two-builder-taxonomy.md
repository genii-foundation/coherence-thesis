## Debt

Volume II presents exactly three builder types as though they were discovered structure, but it does not describe a field sample, mapping method, omitted roles, or counterexamples. The portraits can also flatter the intended in-group and turn an invitation into an identity assignment.

## Evidence

The builder field and taxonomy appear at current lines 895 to 929, followed by a field conclusion at lines 941 to 945. The literary review protects the reportorial stance and the reality of a scattered field. V2-SLOP-26 finds that the exact three-part taxonomy lacks enough method or evidence to claim completeness.

## Paydown criteria

Choose whether the three types are a heuristic, the result of documented field research, or several illustrative profiles. If heuristic, say so directly and add omitted roles and counterexamples where they improve the map. If empirical, document the sample and method. Remove language that flatters readers into a builder identity, while preserving a durable invitation for people already doing relevant work.
