## Debt

Volume IX introduces Land-NFT, SDAC, and the Container as named architectural elements even though the earlier volumes do not establish them and the master ledger does not include them in the canonical stack.

## Evidence

The master ledger admits that Land-NFT and SDAC appear nowhere in Volumes I through VIII. The Container is treated in Volume IX as a formal layer held by The Purposeful Foundation, while the prior corpus uses container mainly as ordinary language. The legal, technical, and governance implications remain undefined.

## Paydown criteria

Decide which terms remain canon. For each retained term, define its mechanism, legal status, relationship to existing layers, safeguards, and first appearance. Remove drafting residue and technology labels whose baggage or precision the architecture cannot support.
