## Debt

The scaling passage turns Scales into cells, communities into a surface and body, and Providence into both connective tissue and a nervous system. The image is memorable and deliberately strange, but it assigns overlapping anatomical functions that later technical prose sometimes treats literally.

## Evidence

The independent literary review approved the image's force. The slop review found that the mixed anatomy could blur metaphor and mechanism as later volumes extend the same body language.

## Paydown criteria

Obtain explicit author approval for the poetic compression, or assign distinct functions to cells, tissue, nervous system, surface, and body across the corpus. Preserve the image without letting it stand as biological evidence.
