## Debt

The master ledger and Volume IX call Proof of Council a validated practice, while Volume III correctly says the minting mechanism remains an unvalidated proposal. The corpus does not distinguish an internal qualification rule from empirical validation.

## Evidence

Volume III now describes only a proposed validation standard. Volume IX says a gathering is validated and can mint COHERENCE without defining the validator, evidence, appeal, failure state, or whether validation has ever occurred.

## Paydown criteria

Define the procedure, authority, evidence, participant consent, appeal, revocation, and failure state. Use separate language for a proposed eligibility rule and completed empirical support, and do not call the mechanism validated before it has passed the named test.
