## Debt

Volume IV presents itself as a builder's manual and repeatedly uses scholarship as proof of feasibility, but it provides no complete bibliography, source-note apparatus, or verified quotation register.

## Evidence

Representative passages at lines 163 to 171, 391 to 409, 795 to 803, 889 to 897, 961 to 969, 1375 to 1383, and 1437 to 1447 cite scholars and summarize research. Lines 1571 and 1609 to 1613 make claims about network states, living systems, and dragon traditions. Several chapter epigraphs are presented as adaptations without exact verification.

## Paydown criteria

Build a complete source register. Verify every quotation and attributed summary against primary sources. Identify evidence strength and distinguish sourced findings, author synthesis, analogy, and scenario. Remove or narrow claims that exceed their sources.
