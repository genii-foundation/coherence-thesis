## Debt

Volume III does not yet settle whether a personal companion is Providence's core strategic direction, one bounded experiment, or a concept that should be removed in favor of facilitated gatherings.

## Evidence

The baseline framed the companion as an immediate and immense market. The revision rejects that inevitability and surfaces privacy, dependency, bystander, coercion, consent, demand, benefit, harm, willingness-to-pay, and unit-economic risks, but its optional status remains an editorial choice rather than approved canon.

## Paydown criteria

Choose the companion's strategic status. Before any pilot or economic claim, conduct consented research, compare simpler alternatives, test relationship outcomes and dependence, establish bystander protections, define the technical safeguards, and build a reviewed willingness-to-pay and unit-economic model. Align every product, financing, market, and invitation passage with that decision.
