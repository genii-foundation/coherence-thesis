## Debt

The source contains exact one-to-three, five-to-eight, and ten-to-fifteen minute orientation labels without a recorded reading or listening method. The figures drift whenever the prose changes.

## Evidence

The labels appear in the Seed, Sprout, and Stem display matter. No source metadata or script explains their reading speed, scope, rounding, or relationship to the generated reader estimates.

## Paydown criteria

Define and automate the method from scoped word counts, label the figures honestly as approximate, or replace them with nonnumeric orientation labels. Verify source and generated reader timing agree.
