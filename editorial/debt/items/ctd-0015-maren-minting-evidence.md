## Debt

The Maren vignettes describe testimony, selected encounters, accepted reflections, commitments, patterns, and all four biometric streams without identifying which evidence actually mints COHERENCE or when that mechanism exists in the roadmap.

## Evidence

Testimony may mean subjective report. The surrounding anti-counterfeiting argument relies on measurable relational change, and Proof of Council is only an ancillary path. Volume III's scenario uses experimental pulse morphology and a coherence record even though its roadmap postpones measurement to Providence 3.0 and accumulated standing to 4.0. The current wording leaves the evidence, minting mechanism, and version ambiguous.

## Paydown criteria

Place each vignette in a named version and time horizon. Decide whether it represents biometric attestation, human testimony, Proof of Council, or a reviewed combination. State which evidence enters a record and which event mints a unit, then align the roadmap and technical chapters without overstating what measurement proves.
