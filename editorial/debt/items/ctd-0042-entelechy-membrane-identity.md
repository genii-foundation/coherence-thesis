## Debt

Volume IX equates Entelechy with the Membrane, while Volume VII uses entelechy as a philosophical account of presencing and Volumes III and the master ledger define the Membrane without that identity.

## Evidence

Volume III calls the Membrane the continuing human practice of attention, introduction, and stewardship. Volume VII uses entelechy for a relation that brings potential into actuality. Volume IX converts Entelechy into the proper name of the matching layer. The shift may be intentional, but the corpus does not explain or authorize it.

## Paydown criteria

Choose whether Entelechy is a philosophical description, a formal layer name, or neither. Define the relationship once, preserve the useful meaning in Volume VII, and align Volumes III and IX plus the master ledger.
